First Semester Sophomore Year

Meaning and Metaphor

One of my favorite illustration projects from Sophomore year was the LISTVENTORY project. The idea behind it was to choose an object and create 20 different and unique versions of it.

Naturally I chose potions, and from there my ideas soared. I created different bottle designs that would mimic the effects the potions would have. My ideas ranged from original ones like floating and water breathing, to plays on real world products like Starbucks and Axe body spray. See if you can guess them all...
